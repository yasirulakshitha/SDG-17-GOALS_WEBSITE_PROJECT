Targets!....

SAFE AND AFFORDABLE DRINKING WATER


By 2030, achieve universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all

END OPEN DEFECATION AND PROVIDE ACCESS TO SANITATION AND HYGIENE

By 2030, ensure universal access to sanitation and hygiene, including an end to open defecation, with a focus on addressing the needs of women, girls, and vulnerable populations.

IMPROVE WATER QUALITY, WASTEWATER TREATMENT AND SAFE REUSE


By 2030, enhance water quality by reducing pollution, halving untreated wastewater, and significantly increasing global recycling and safe reuse.

INCREASE WATER-USE EFFICIENCY AND ENSURE FRESHWATER SUPPLIES


By 2030, increase water-use efficiency, ensure sustainable freshwater supply, and reduce water scarcity for all.

IMPLEMENT INTEGRATED WATER RESOURCES MANAGEMENT


By 2030, implement integrated water resources management at all levels, including through transboundary cooperation as appropriate.

PROTECT AND RESTORE WATER-RELATED ECOSYSTEMS


By 2020, protect and restore water-related ecosystems, including mountains, forests, wetlands, rivers, aquifers and lakes.

EXPAND WATER AND SANITATION SUPPORT TO DEVELOPING COUNTRIES


By 2030, enhance global aid for water activities in developing nations, focusing on harvesting, desalination, efficiency, and recycling technologies.

SUPPORT LOCAL ENGAGEMENT IN WATER AND SANITATION MANAGEMENT


Support and strengthen the participation of local communities in improving water and sanitation management.
